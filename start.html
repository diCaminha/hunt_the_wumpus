Welcome to Hunt the Wumpus: Online

Created by: Austin, Denis, and Vika

Rules of the Game:

  1. The overall goal of the game is to find your way through a maze of caves and find the Wumpus without dying.

  2. You start with 5 arrows

  3. You may choose a room or choose to fire an arrow on every turn

  4. The Wumpus can only be killed if he is hit by an arrow

  5. An arrow can travel up to 3 rooms

  6. If you pick a room that has bats, they will randomly teleport you to another room in the maze

  7. If you pick a room that is a bottomless pit. GAME OVER.

  8. When you shoot an arrow, the rooms you pick MUST be connected to each other

Choose your fate:

1 = Choose a room
2 = Shoot an arrow
